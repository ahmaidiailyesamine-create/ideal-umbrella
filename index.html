Our History

Founded in 2021 by a group of passionate players and coaches, CAFJT Basketball Club started from a single outdoor court in Relizane. Over two decades, we've grown into one of the city's most respected basketball organizations, producing players who compete at regional and national levels. Our journey is rooted in community, discipline, and an unrelenting love of the game.

Mission

To develop complete athletes and better human beings through basketball. We provide world-class coaching, a safe and inclusive environment, and a clear pathway from youth development through to elite competition — ensuring every player reaches their full potential on and off the court.

Vision

To become Algeria's leading basketball club — recognized for producing elite talent, championing community engagement, and setting the standard for player welfare, coaching excellence, and competitive achievement at every level of the game.
